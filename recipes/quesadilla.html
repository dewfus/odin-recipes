Quesadilla

Description

A Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla, filled primarily with cheese, and sometimes meat,
spices, and other things.

Ingredients

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Grated cheese, sharp cheddar
  • Olive oil
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Chicken
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put tortilla in frying pan.
  2. Put ingredients on the tortilla.
  3. Heat to desired doneness.
  4. Fold it like a taco.
  5. Done.
