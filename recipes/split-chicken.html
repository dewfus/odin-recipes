Split Chicken Breasts

Description

A chicken recipe that can be used to cook chicken in a smoker, or instant pot,
or however you prefer.

Ingredients

  • Bone-in, skin-on chicken breasts
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and cajun seasoning.
  2. Pour chicken broth and add chicken into a pressure cooker. Close and
    select high pressure and set timer for 10 minutes. Allow 10 to 15
    minutes to build pressure.
  3. Release pressure, about 10 minutes. Release remaining pressure,
    about 5 minutes. Remove lid.
  4. Set oven rack and preheat broiler.
  5. Place chicken on baking sheet and broil until nicely browned,
    about 4 minutes.
